Scrambled eggs with cheese

Let's make a delightful meal with one of natures most nutrient rich resources. And today we're going to scramble them, because doing it any other way is gross! We'll also throw in some cheese to add an extra dimension to the flavor. Hello Gordon!

Ingredients

  • 4 large white eggs, or however many you'd prefer
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • Half a cup of triple cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat a nonstick pan to medium heat. While preheating, pour a small amount of olive oil to heat with the pan
  2. Crack all the eggs into a mixing bowl and add the salt and pepper, then beat with a fork or whisk.
  3. Once beaten, pour the eggs into the pan, then sprinkle in a healthy amount of shredded cheese. Continually stir the eggs around while they curdle. This insures a fluffy scrambled egg.
  4. Once it is to a desired consistency, remove from the heat and serve. Add salt and pepper if desired.
